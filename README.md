Wordsearch
==========

Generate Wordsearches in Ruby

Outputs a grid like: 

" V  G  V  F  A  F  D "
" V  O  W  D  R  M  P "
" A  D  Y  Q  O  Y  X "
" T  U  X  N  S  V  Q "
" P  A  K  K  J  T  J "
" P  E  F  Z  T  Y  T "
" Y  C  O  O  L  J  G "

Which cunningly conceals a few user-defined words (eg. Dog, Monkey & Cool). 

All directions (including diagonals) are supported.
